How It Works

An autonomous bot runs on a Raspberry Pi in a living room somewhere. Every 12 hours it wakes up, scrapes Reddit for misogynistic quotes, runs them through a two-layer AI security guard, generates typographic artwork with kinetic animations, uploads everything to IPFS, mints NFTs on Base, lists them on the marketplace, and posts to Bluesky. No human touches the pipeline.

When someone buys an NFT, the hateful quote transforms into an empowering message about women. The hate becomes strength. Proceeds are split: 50% to Refuge (UK domestic abuse charity), 30% to the artist, 20% to keep the bot running.

Animation Styles

Each NFT is assigned one of 8 kinetic animation styles. The scramble effect is the signature — random characters resolve into the hateful text, forcing you to watch the words form.

Scramble
Random ASCII resolves into text. The signature style.
Typewriter
Characters typed one by one with occasional typos.
Redacted
Pink bars lift to reveal scrambled text underneath.
Flicker
Text flickers like a dying neon sign.
Heartbeat
Text pulses with RGB split and shockwaves.
Corruption
Hex data infected by the quote like a virus.
Interactive
Characters flee from your cursor. Click to shatter.
ASCII Art
Giant letters made from misogynistic slur fill chars.

About Refuge

Refuge is the UK's largest domestic abuse organisation, supporting over 6,000 women and children on any given day. They run the National Domestic Abuse Helpline (0808 2000 247), a network of safe houses across the country, and services that help women rebuild their lives after abuse.

50% of all proceeds from MISOGYNY.EXE go directly to Refuge. They are not affiliated with this project. We just think they do vital work.
